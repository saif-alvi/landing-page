Welcome to the Student Learning Centre!

The Sheldon & Tracy Levy Student Learning Centre (SLC) is an iconic structure in the heart of Toronto and the symbolic “front door” of the Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University) campus—a world-class learning destination for the university community.

SLC Floors

The Sun, floor 5 with large study spaces and several private rooms for booking.

The Beach, floor 6 with an open auditorium unique study space modeled after a beach.

The Sky, floor 8 the top floor with large open collaborative study spaces with an amazing view of downtown Toronto.

The Valley, floor 1 a beautiful entrace with modern seating arrangements and space to serve as Ryerson's communal center.

“Eh, all of this is not as important as the students enjoying the building and so I’m happy to do this because you have to recognize the donors and the support you got in a formal way, but the real gift was when the students said ‘I love it.’”

- Sheldon Levy
